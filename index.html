LEMON Restaurant

Welcome to Lemon retaurant Ramallah where flavor takes center stage!🍋

LEMON first opened its doors in 2008 in Amman Jordan and it soon became a regional favorite brand. Famous for its delicious signature dishes, friendly staff, extensive beverage list and shisha, LEMON keeps visitors entertained with TV screens featuring live sports tournaments, card and board games.

👉 Tokyo Street,Ein Munjid, Ramallah, Palestine
